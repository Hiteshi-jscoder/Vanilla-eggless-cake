# Vanilla-eggless-cake
1. Add 1 cup curd 1 cup sugar and mix them.
2. Add baking soda and baking powder in curd mixture and mix. Keep this mixture aside for 5 minutes.
3. After 5 minutes bubbles will form. Then add vanilla essence and oil and mix them.
4. Then add maida in the above mixture in 2-3 batches and mix. Then add milk and mix.
5. Now, the batter for cake is ready. pour it in a container and bake for 30-35 minutes at 180 degree celcius temperature.
6. Vanilla eggless cake is ready.
